Design rationale
Operator's first-glance answer ("what am I holding right now?") now resolves above the fold on both desktop and mobile. Risk + trades + daily-kill are visual gauges — at a glance the operator sees the daily-kill bar at 107% (red, breached), while the trades gauge is at 47% and risk at 20%. The kill-switch banner only renders when active and is impossible to miss. The legacy market-index marquee is demoted to a small secondary footer. Mobile ticker matrix becomes a card stack (no horizontal scroll). Position progress bars give an instant sense of where each trade is in the RR=2.5 risk/reward arc; the 1R marker on the bar makes BE-arm threshold obvious. Broker P&L sits next to paper P&L with a delta chip whenever they diverge.
Color + state legend
WARMUP blue · ticker waiting for OR lock
OR LOCKED amber · transient state
ARMED green · eligible for breakout admission
IN POS bright green · position currently open
CLOSED gray · position exited, may re-enter
BLOCKED red · gated out (VIX / earnings / gap / blocklist / range / daily kill)
